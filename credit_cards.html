Eligibility Criteria

Eligibility criteria for a gold loan are simple — you should be at least 18 years old, own the gold jewelry being pledged, and provide basic KYC documents for verification.

Age Requirement :

  • You must be at least 18 years old (some banks may ask for a higher minimum age).

Income Requirement :

  • You need to have a minimum income to show you can repay the credit card bills.

Credit Score :

  • A good credit score improves your chances of approval and better card benefits

Residential Proof:

  • You must provide valid address/residency proof as per the bank’s requirement.
Even if you meet the criteria, credit card approval depends on the issuer’s assessment. Different cards have different rules, so always check terms and conditions before applying.

Documents Required to Apply for a Credit Card

Documents Required to Apply for a Credit Card are the essential papers you need to submit, such as identity proof, address proof, income proof, and recent photographs, to verify your eligibility and complete the application process

Identity Proof :

  • Aadhaar, PAN, Driving License, Passport, or Voter ID to confirm your identity.

Address Proof :

  • Electricity/telephone bill, Aadhaar, or any valid document to verify your residence.

Annual ITR (Self-Employed) :

  • For self-employed people, Income Tax Returns show your income details.

Latest Salary Slips (Salaried):

  • Recent salary slips prove your monthly income and repayment ability.

Different Types of Credit Cards in India

Different types of credit cards in India include rewards cards, cashback cards, travel cards, fuel cards, shopping cards, lifestyle cards, business cards, and secured cards—each designed to suit different spending habits and financial needs.

Shopping Credit Cards :

  • Best for frequent shoppers; earn cashback or reward points on purchases that can be redeemed for products, travel, or vouchers.

Lifestyle Credit Cards :

  • Perfect for dining, entertainment, and retail; get discounts, cashback, and reward points on lifestyle spends.

Fuel Credit Cards :

  • Ideal for regular fuel buyers; save money on petrol/diesel and also enjoy lifestyle benefits.

Rewards Credit Cards :

  • Earn reward points, cashback, or miles on every spend; redeem them for travel, shopping, or vouchers.

Travel Credit Cards :

  • Great for frequent travelers; get discounts on flight bookings, hotels, and earn miles or reward points.

Features & Benefits of Credit Cards

Credit cards provide convenient cashless transactions, flexible repayment options, and access to rewards like cashback, discounts, or travel miles. They also help build credit history while offering perks such as EMI conversion, online shopping security, and emergency fund access.
