Home Loan Eligibility

Age :

  • must be 21 years or older at the time of applying.
  • Maximum age allowed at loan maturity: 65 years.
  • Salaried: 21 to 58 years.
  • Self-Employed: 25 to 65 years.

Income :

  • Salaried: Minimum monthly income of ₹10,000.
  • Self-Employed: Minimum annual income of ₹2,00,000.

Employment :

  • Salaried: Minimum 2–3 years of work experience in an MNC, private, or public limited company.
  • Self-Employed: Minimum 3 years of experience in the current business/profession.

Credit Score :

  • A credit score of 750 or above is considered good for quick approval.

2. Documentation Required

2.1 For Salaried Individuals
  • Form 16
  • Employee Identity Card
  • Last 3 months’ Salary Slips
  • Last 6 months’ Bank Statement
  • Proof of Employment Duration
2.2 For Self-Employed Individuals
  • PAN Card
  • Trade License / Partnership Deed
  • Memorandum & Articles of Association
  • Import Export Code / SEBI / ROC Certificate (if applicable)
  • Profession-specific license (Doctors, Consultants, etc.)
  • Shop / Factory / Establishment Registration Certificate
  • Business Address Proof
  • Latest Financial Statements audited by a CA
  • Profit & Loss Account, Balance Sheet
  • Last 6 months’ Bank Statement
2.3 Documents Required from all Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) Applicants
  • Income Proof Documents
  • Valid Property Papers
  • Sale Deed & Stamped Agreement of Sale
  • Allotment Letter
  • No Objection Certificate (from housing society / builder / authority)
  • Possession Certificate
  • Land Tax Receipt
  • Construction Cost Estimate (if applicable)
  • Bank Statement of Payments made to Seller/Builder
  • Payment Receipts
  • Share Certificate (if resale property)
  • Occupancy Certificate
