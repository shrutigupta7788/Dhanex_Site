Gold Loan

Eligibility Criteria

  • You should be at least 18 years old to apply.
  • The gold you pledge must be owned by you.
  • The gold should meet the purity standards set by the lender.
  • You need to provide a valid government ID for verification.

Documents Required to Apply for Gold Loan

Identity & Signature Proof:

  • Aadhaar, PAN, Passport, Voter ID, Driving License, or Govt. Employee ID.

Address Proof :

  • Rent Agreement, Bank Statement, Passport, Voter ID, Driving License, or utility bills (electricity, water, telephone, credit card bill, property tax).

Security :

  • Two post-dated cheques.

Photographs:

  • Passport-size photos of the applicant.

How to Apply for Gold Loan at Ruloans?

You can apply for a Gold Loan at Ruloans easily by visiting their website or nearest branch, filling out the application form, submitting your KYC documents along with the gold, and getting funds disbursed quickly after valuation.

Choose a Lender :

  • Pick a trusted bank, NBFC, or gold loan company.

Check Eligibility :

  • Make sure you meet the lender’s age and gold purity requirements.

Apply Online/Visit Branch :

  • Apply through the lender’s website, app, or nearest branch.

Gold Evaluation :

  • Your gold will be checked for purity and value to decide the loan amount (some lenders even offer doorstep service).
